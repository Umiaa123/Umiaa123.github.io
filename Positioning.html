90 Days to CEO by Rochelle Graham-Campbell

The book "90 Days to CEO" by Rochelle Graham-Campbell is about entrepreneurship and how to handle the pitfalls and success from your business and the author speaks about her life and struggles as well.

Rochelle Graham-Campbell is a mother, wife, and a successful haircare business owner that has her products from her line called "Alikay Naturals" that are sold in major retail stores such as Target, Walmart, Rite-aid, CVS, and more globally in 26 countries and counting.

This book to me is like talking to a mentor, and guiding me to becoming the best businesswoman I can be since I myself started a bussiness and wants to learn and know more about the beauty industry.
